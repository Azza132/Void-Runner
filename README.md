VOID RUNNER
How to Play
Created by Aaron Sermon

----------------------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

Void Runner is a browser-based arcade dodge game. You pilot a ship
through an endless asteroid field, collecting stars and surviving
as long as possible. The longer you last, the harder it gets.

Open asteroid-dodge.html in any modern web browser to play instantly.
No installation, no internet connection, and no setup required.

----------------------------------------------------------------

OBJECTIVE

Dodge falling asteroids. Collect glowing yellow stars for bonus
points. Survive as long as you can and beat your best score.

----------------------------------------------------------------

CONTROLS

You can use either the keyboard or the mouse — or both at once.

  Arrow Left / A    Move ship left
  Arrow Right / D   Move ship right
  Mouse             Move the mouse over the game area to guide
                    your ship to that position

----------------------------------------------------------------

SCORING

  Survival          You earn points automatically just for staying
                    alive. The higher your sector, the more points
                    you earn per second.

  Yellow Stars      Each star you fly through is worth 50 points.
                    Stars fall from the top of the screen and
                    disappear if you miss them, so chase them
                    when it is safe to do so.

----------------------------------------------------------------

LIVES & DAMAGE

You start with 3 lives, shown as triangles in the bottom left.

When you collide with an asteroid you lose one life and become
briefly invincible, giving you a moment to recover. Your ship
will flash to indicate this invincibility window.

Lose all 3 lives and it is game over.

----------------------------------------------------------------

SECTORS (DIFFICULTY)

The game is divided into sectors. Every 500 points you advance
to the next sector, which increases the speed and spawn rate
of asteroids. The current sector is displayed at the bottom
of the screen.

  Sector 1    Starting pace. Learn the patterns.
  Sector 2    Noticeably faster. More asteroids on screen.
  Sector 3+   Increasingly chaotic. Survival becomes difficult.

There is no upper limit. The game keeps escalating for as long
as you can hold on.

----------------------------------------------------------------

ASTEROID TYPES

Most asteroids are grey and worth avoiding. Occasionally a red
or pink glowing asteroid will appear. These behave the same way
but are slightly more visible — treat them as a warning to
give yourself extra clearance.

----------------------------------------------------------------

BEST SCORE

Your best score is saved automatically in your browser and
persists between sessions. It is displayed in the top right
corner next to your current score. If you beat it, the game
over screen will highlight it as a new best.

----------------------------------------------------------------

TIPS

- Do not hug the edges. Keeping to the centre gives you more
  room to dodge in either direction.

- Stars are worth chasing but not at any cost. A risky star
  is not worth losing a life over.

- Learn to read incoming patterns a second or two ahead rather
  than reacting at the last moment.

- Your ship eases toward your target position rather than
  snapping instantly. Account for this slight lag, especially
  at higher speeds.

- In later sectors, avoid staying still. Keep moving in small
  side-to-side sweeps to reduce the chance of an asteroid
  landing directly on you.

----------------------------------------------------------------

TECHNICAL NOTES

The game runs entirely in a single HTML file with no external
dependencies beyond Google Fonts. It works offline once the
file has been opened. Best scores are saved using your
browser's local storage and will persist as long as you use
the same browser on the same device.

Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
